Section 01

Overview

At Mirabella Ceylon, your satisfaction is our highest priority. We stand behind every certified gemstone we sell and offer a straightforward, no-hassle return process to give you complete confidence in your purchase.

You may return any eligible item within 14 days of the delivery date for a full refund or exchange — no questions asked. The item must be in its original, unworn condition and accompanied by the original gemstone certificate.

Our Promise: Every gemstone sold by Mirabella Ceylon is certified by the National Gem & Jewellery Authority of Sri Lanka (NGJA) and/or GIA. If your item arrives in a condition that does not match its certification or description, we will arrange a full refund and cover all return shipping costs.
Section 02

Eligible Items

The following items are eligible for return and refund:

  • Loose certified gemstones returned in original condition with the gemstone certificate intact
  • Jewellery items that are unworn, unaltered, and returned with original packaging and documentation
  • Items that were damaged during shipping or do not match the description on the product page
  • Items where a material error was made in the listing (e.g. incorrect weight, origin, or treatment status)

To qualify, the item must be:

  • Returned within 14 calendar days of the confirmed delivery date
  • In its original, unused condition — not worn, set, drilled, or altered in any way
  • Accompanied by the original gemstone certificate (NGJA and/or GIA)
  • Packed securely to prevent damage during return transit
Section 03

Non-Eligible Items

The following items are not eligible for return or refund:

  • Custom and bespoke orders — gemstones sourced, cut, or mounted to your specific requirements cannot be returned unless defective
  • Items returned after 14 days from the delivery date
  • Items that have been worn, set, drilled, resized, or altered in any way
  • Items with missing, altered, or tampered certificates — the original NGJA/GIA certificate must be returned with the item
  • Items damaged due to buyer mishandling, improper storage, or negligence
  • Sale or clearance items marked as final sale at the time of purchase

If you are unsure whether your item is eligible, please contact us via WhatsApp before sending the return. We are always happy to assist.

Section 04

How to Initiate a Return

To start a return, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact us within 14 days of delivery via WhatsApp at +94 71 845 6999 or by email at returns@mirabelaceylon.com
  2. Provide your order number (found in your confirmation email or WhatsApp thread)
  3. State the reason for your return — a brief explanation helps us improve our service, but is not required for approval
  4. Send photos of the item and its certificate so we can verify the condition before authorising the return
  5. Once approved, we will provide you with the return shipping address and packing instructions
  6. Pack the item securely and ship it back using a trackable, insured courier — we recommend the same service used for delivery
  7. Share the tracking number with us via WhatsApp once dispatched
Important: Do not send returns without prior authorisation. Unauthorised returns may not be processed or may incur additional handling fees.
Section 05

Refund Process

Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we will notify you of the outcome within 2 business days.

If your return is approved:

  • Your refund will be processed to the original payment method used at checkout
  • Bank transfers and mobile wallet refunds (FriMi / eZ Cash) are typically completed within 3 – 5 business days
  • Credit card refunds may take 5 – 10 business days depending on your card issuer
  • PayPal refunds are usually completed within 3 – 5 business days
  • The full purchase price is refunded — we do not deduct restocking fees

If your return is not approved:

  • We will contact you with the reason and arrange to return the item to you at no extra cost
  • If you believe the decision is incorrect, you may escalate by emailing support@mirabelaceylon.com
Section 06

Exchange Policy

If you would prefer to exchange your item for another gemstone rather than receive a refund, we are happy to assist. Exchanges follow the same eligibility rules as returns.

  • Contact us via WhatsApp within 14 days of delivery to discuss exchange options
  • If the replacement item is of higher value, you will be asked to pay the difference
  • If the replacement item is of lower value, we will refund the difference to your original payment method
  • Exchanges are subject to current stock availability — we will do our best to find the closest match to your preference
  • Custom sourcing is available if the exact gem you want is not currently in stock — contact us for details
Section 07

International Returns

We welcome returns from customers worldwide. The following terms apply to international return shipments:

  • Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the item was damaged, defective, or incorrectly described
  • We recommend using a trackable, insured international courier such as DHL, FedEx, or UPS — we cannot accept responsibility for items lost in transit
  • Please mark the return parcel as "Returned Goods — No Commercial Value" on the customs declaration to avoid import duties. Failure to do so may result in customs charges that cannot be refunded
  • Customs duties or import taxes incurred on the original shipment are non-refundable
  • Please contact us before sending an international return so we can provide the correct documentation and return address
Section 08

Damaged or Incorrect Items

We take exceptional care with packaging and quality control. However, if your order arrives damaged or incorrect, we will make it right — immediately and at no cost to you.

If your item arrives damaged:

  • Please photograph the damaged packaging and item before opening fully — this helps us file a claim with the courier
  • Contact us within 48 hours of delivery via WhatsApp with the photos and your order number
  • We will arrange a full replacement or refund and cover all return shipping costs

If you received the wrong item:

  • Contact us immediately with your order number and photos of the item received
  • We will dispatch the correct item as soon as possible and provide a prepaid return label for the incorrect item
  • You will not be charged for return shipping under any circumstances when the error is ours
Fully Insured Shipping: All orders from Mirabella Ceylon are shipped fully insured for their declared value. In the rare event of loss or damage during transit, you are fully covered.
