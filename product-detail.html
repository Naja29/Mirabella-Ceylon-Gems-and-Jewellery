About This Stone

Ceylon Sapphires are celebrated worldwide for their exceptional blue hue — a vibrant, velvety royal blue that distinguishes them from sapphires found in any other country. This particular stone exhibits a perfectly saturated colour with no colour zoning under standard lighting.

The oval brilliant cut has been expertly executed to maximise brilliance while retaining weight, resulting in a lively, well-proportioned gem with strong optical performance from all viewing angles.
